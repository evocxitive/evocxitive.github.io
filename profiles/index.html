Photographer Profiles

Check out our featured photographers of the week!

Sofie Sund

Sofie is a well-known photographer from Norway. Her style is very elegant and simple, yet full of depth.
She mainly takes self-portraits, though often displaying the beauty of forests, fields, and houses in Norway
in the background. She occasionally participates or creates photography contests on Instagram. She takes her
photographers with a Nikon camera. Check out her website here.

Frosted Productions

The photographer at Frosted Productions, Amber Bauerle, is a master in her profession. She is a hardworking,
dedicated photographer that specializes in children and fashion photography. She is very skilled with Photoshop
and her photos are a combination of sophistication and edge. You can check her out here.
