About Us

The Omphalos is a website dedicated to both aspiring and professional photographers all around the world. Here, different photographers are featured each week, and each photographer brings their own style to the website. At the Omphalos, we are ever inspired by the creative endeavours that are taken by the talented people. We hope that by providing a serene environment like the Omphalos, others are also encouraged to pursue and achieve their artistic goals.
