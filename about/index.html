About Us

The Omphalos is a website dedicated to both aspiring and professional
photographers all around the world. Here, different photographers are
featured each week, and each photographer brings their own style to the website.
At the Omphalos, we are ever inspired by the creative endeavours that are taken by
these incredibly talented photographers. We hope that by providing a serene
environment like the Omphalos, others are also encouraged to pursue and
achieve their artistic goals.

The Omphalos was originally started in 2011 as a Greek photo blog by Aaron
Walter (hence, the Greek title, "Omphalos"). Since then, it has expanded to include
a wide array of different photos displaying culture, aesthetic, and creativity from
everywhere in the world. The photographers featured each week come from a variety of
unique backgrounds and cultures, and all add to the diverse community that is the Omphalos.

Here at the Omphalos, our goal is to provide as many people as possible with the
inspiration and motivation to challenge themselves creatively. In a world that is
often dominated by the hustle and bustle of busy schedules and never-ending errands,
we want to provide those who wish to study the arts a beginning place to do so.
Every year, we provide over two hundred scholarships for those entering post
-secondary education seeking a degree in art. All this is possible only with the
generous donations and help from sponsors, and you.

We hope you enjoy your time here at the Omphalos!
